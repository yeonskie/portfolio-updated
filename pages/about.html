ABOUT ME
Personal (Informal)

I've found myself paralyzed by the phrase, "tell me about yourself". It should be simple, since people say no one knows you better than you know yourself, but I've found the exact opposite to be true.

There are so many sides of me that are a reflection of who I am that it's hard to catch all the pieces and place them together before the next moment passes and I ultimately forget that little moments make up who I am.

If you're interested, I have a few different ways to addressing these different pieces of myself, and if you click on each individual tab, you'll get a glimpse of the sides I present to the world.
