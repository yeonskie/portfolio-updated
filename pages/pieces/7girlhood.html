21YearsofGirlhood.com
- Senior Thesis Project

21YearsofGirlhood explores the digital age of girlhood and its effects on sexuality, mental health, and addiction with key scavenger-hunt-like elements to explore the identity of a young Asian girl growing up in the twenty-first century.

Girlhood is a transitional and pivotal time in self-discovery and reflection that lingers long into adulthood. With every passing minute, year, and decade that passes, the female experience and what it means to be a girl has been reshaped to fit a constantly changing society.
