LUCKY ONE: Rewriting the Model Minority Myth (2024)
- Collaboration with Rohan Nair, Zoraz Haseeb, Emily Richardson
- VCUArts Undergraduate Research Grant (Spring 2024)

LUCKY ONE: Rewriting the Model Minority Myth is a multi-modal, community-focused zine, intended to be distributed as both a physical booklet and an interactive website.

The effect of the model minority myth on all racial groups is devastating. It dilutes all minority racial groups to stereotypes, pits them against one another, and perpetuates white supremacy.

Through a prompt-based submission process, LUCKY ONE showcases the hidden breadth and depth of the Asian experience, emphasizing the multi-dimensionality of identity as a larger concept and drawing a clear division between what is real and what is myth.
