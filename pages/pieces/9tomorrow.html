To Myself, Tomorrow
To Myself, Tomorrow is a book dedicated to eating disorder recovery. It was made for people recovering in their own disorders, family of people with EDs, or people who would like to know more about them. It is meant to be informational yet still visually pleasing in contrast to other books of this subject matter. The book includes personal statements, articles, and quotes from various sources about recovery.
