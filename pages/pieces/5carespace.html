Carespace
A 3-D space designed for the care and use of psychiatrist hospital patients.

Psychatric hospital rooms are often designed for convenience, if there is any design at all. Patients spend upwards of 12+ hours in a cramped, poorly utilized space that offers little comfort for patients already in distress.

There was an emphasis on redesigning a small, cramped space as these rooms often are. Furniture can be collapsed or hidden among the spaces, allowing for easy storage and clean up. Each area of the room allows for some privacy or safe space as well as community spaces and furniture.
